Helvetica Neue vs Neue Haas Grotesk

History of each typeface

Neue Haas Grotesk

Created in 1957, later renamed as Helvetica in 1960.

Helvetica Neue

Redesigned and released in 1983

Comparison

Similarities

The both are sans-serif typefaces

Differences

Helvetica is wider and "f" has slight angle less round 0

Helvetica, more closed @ and italic look

Z has more horizontal angle

Haas is taller and smaller tilde, Æ more squarish
